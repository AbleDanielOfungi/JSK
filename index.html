    Frequently Asked Questions

    We understand that you may have questions about our services and how we operate. To make things easier, we've compiled a list of the most frequently asked questions. Whether you're curious about our service options, turnaround times, or how we handle your garments, you'll find the answers you need right here. If you don't see your question listed, feel free to reach out to us directly—we're always here to help!

    1. What services does JoseMark Laundry offer?

    We offer a range of laundry services, including Wash and Fold (with free ironing), Dry Cleaning, and Ironing services. We handle all types of garments and fabrics, ensuring they are cleaned and cared for with the highest standards.

    2. How do I place an order for laundry services?

    You can place an order by contacting us directly via phone, email, or WhatsApp. You can also visit our location for drop-off and pick-up services. We aim to make the process as convenient as possible for you

    3. Do you offer pickup and delivery services?

    Yes, we offer pickup and delivery services to make your laundry experience even more convenient. Please contact us to schedule a pickup time that works best for you.

    4. How long does it take to process my laundry?

    Our standard turnaround time is 24-48 hours, depending on the service. However, we also offer express services if you need your items cleaned more quickly. Just let us know your needs, and we’ll do our best to accommodate them.

    5. What is the difference between Wash and Fold and Dry Cleaning?

    Wash and Fold is for everyday clothing that can be machine washed and dried. This service includes washing, drying, folding, and free ironing. Dry Cleaning is a specialized service for delicate or high-end garments that require gentle cleaning methods, which do not involve water, to maintain fabric quality.

    6. Can you handle special fabrics or items with specific care instructions?

    Absolutely! We have experience handling a wide variety of fabrics, including delicate and specialty items. Please provide any specific care instructions when you drop off your laundry, and we will ensure they are followed.
